Features:

  1. Can be opened under Regular Savings or Current Account Category.
  2. Account can be opened by Individuals (singly or jointly) only.
  3. Offered in PKR only.
  4. No minimum balance requirement.
  5. Maximum Withdrawal (debit) allowed in a calendar month is limited to 500,000 per month also maximum deposit (credit) balance shall not exceed Rs. 500,000.
  6. Foreign outward remittance is not allowed under this account.
  7. For Asaan savings account profit calculated on monthly average balance & paid out on monthly basis.
  8. 24/7 call center with complete Tele- banking services.
