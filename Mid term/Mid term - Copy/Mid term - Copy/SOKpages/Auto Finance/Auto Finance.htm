Procedure

  • Approval time will be depend on case to case
  • After auto financing case approval the customer have to go to manufacturer to select his vehicle.
  • Bank will pay the financing amount in the form of P.O (Pay order)
  • The manufacturer will give him the vehicle delivery time. Tracker must be installed at showroom before delivery of vehicle.
  • Bank Finance Autos on (Musharka & Ijara basis), in which first Contract is Musharka (Partnership) then Ijara (Rent) will be applicable to the customer.

Delivery:

  • Deliver time also will be depend on case to case
  • If Customer selects his own Manufacturer to purchase a car so, bank is not liable in case of late delivery.
  • Documentations regarding Insurance & Tracker installation must be done before delivery.
  • Customer can avail Delivery Acceptance form from Showroom

Registration:

  • Manufacturer will provide the sale certificate and invoice to the Customer.
  • Vendor will responsible for registration.
  • Vendor will provide the number plate and running part to the customer and original book and copy of running part to the SC. (Its take 60 working days almost about 2 Month after delivery)

Requirement:

  1. Copy of Primary applicant’s CNIC.
  2. Copy of Co-applicant’s CNIC (If applicable).
  3. Two passport size photographs.
  4. Latest salary slip/ Proof of income.
  5. 6 Months Bank Statement (If applicable).
  6. Bank Statement (If applicable).
